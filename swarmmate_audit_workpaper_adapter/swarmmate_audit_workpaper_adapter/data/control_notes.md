# Control notes

## Cash and bank reconciliation
- Bank reconciliations are prepared monthly by the staff accountant.
- November and December reconciliations were not signed off by a reviewer until the following month.
- One reconciling item older than 45 days remained open at year end.

## Revenue cycle
- Sales orders are approved in the ERP system, but support for custom-order pricing overrides is retained in email rather than in a central workflow.
- Credit memos above $25,000 require controller approval.

## Inventory
- Cycle counts were performed for high-value raw materials during the year.
- Obsolescence reserve methodology was updated in Q4 using a manual spreadsheet.
