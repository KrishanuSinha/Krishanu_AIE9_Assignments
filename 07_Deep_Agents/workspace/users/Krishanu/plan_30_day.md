## üîÑ How to Adapt When You Struggle

### **If Energy is Low**:
- Reduce exercise intensity by 20%
- Add iron-rich foods (spinach, sesame seeds)
- Check meal timing - eat breakfast within 1 hour of waking
- Increase rest between exercise sets
- Focus on gentle morning movement

### **If Sleep is Poor**:
- Move dinner 30 minutes earlier
- Reduce evening liquid intake
- Add magnesium-rich foods (pumpkin seeds, leafy greens)
- Extend pre-sleep routine to 45 minutes
- Try legs-up-the-wall pose before bed

### **If Stress is High**:
- Add extra 5-minute breathing breaks during the day
- Increase adaptogenic herbs (tulsi tea, ashwagandha)
- Choose gentle, restorative exercise over high intensity
- Practice loving-kindness meditation
- Journal worries to "park" them before sleep

### **If You Miss a Day**:
- No guilt - just restart the next day
- Do a 5-minute version instead of skipping entirely  
- Focus on one area (just nutrition or just movement)
- Remember: progress over perfection
- Celebrate what you DID accomplish
