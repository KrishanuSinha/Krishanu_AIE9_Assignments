# üèÉ‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è MOVEMENT PLAN (3x per week)

## Weekly Structure & Progression

### Week 1: Foundation Building
**Focus**: Establishing routine, learning proper form, building base fitness

#### Workout Schedule (Monday/Wednesday/Friday)
- **Monday - Workout A**: Cardio + Core (30 min)
- **Wednesday - Workout B**: Full Body Strength (30 min)
- **Friday - Workout C**: Movement + Flexibility (30 min)

#### Sample Week 1 Workouts

**Monday - Cardio + Core**
- Warm-up: 5 min marching, arm swings, hip circles
- Main: 20 min alternating walking in place, step touches, modified jumping jacks, core work
- Cool-down: 5 min gentle stretching

**Wednesday - Full Body Strength**
- Warm-up: 5 min joint rotations and light movement
- Main: 20 min circuit (2 sets each):
  * Wall push-ups: 8-12 reps
  * Chair-assisted squats: 10-15 reps
  * Modified lunges: 5 each leg
  * Modified planks: 20-30 sec holds
- Cool-down: 5 min full body stretching

**Friday - Movement + Flexibility**
- Gentle yoga flow: 10 min
- Flexibility sequence: 8 min
- Stress-relief breathing: 4 min
- Relaxation: 5 min body scan meditation

### Week 2: Consistency & Confidence
- Increase cardio intervals to 3-4 minutes
- Add 2-3 more reps to strength exercises
- Introduce resistance bands if available
- Extend flexibility holds to 45-60 seconds

### Week 3: Progressive Challenge
- Introduce HIIT intervals: 30 seconds work, 30 seconds rest
- Progress to full push-ups and unassisted squats (if able)
- Add combination movements (squat to overhead reach)
- Include more challenging yoga poses

### Week 4: Peak & Integration
- Peak intensity with 45-second work intervals
- Combine exercises into circuits
- Add small plyometric elements (quick steps, light jumps)
- Challenge balance and coordination

## Rest Day Activities (4 per week)
Choose 1-2 daily:
- 10-15 min gentle walk
- 5-10 min breathing exercises or meditation
- 10 min light stretching
- Stress-relief activities (reading, music, creative hobbies)
- Self-care practices (warm bath, journaling)

# üí° SUCCESS TIPS

1. **Start Small**: It's better to do 5 minutes consistently than 20 minutes sporadically
2. **Stack Habits**: Attach new habits to existing routines (e.g., breathwork while coffee brews)
3. **Plan for Obstacles**: Identify your likely challenges and prepare solutions
4. **Celebrate Wins**: Acknowledge progress daily, no matter how small
5. **Stay Flexible**: Life happens - adapt rather than abandon
6. **Focus on How You Feel**: Energy, mood, and sleep quality matter more than perfect execution

## Red Flags to Watch For
- Extreme fatigue or persistent soreness (reduce intensity)
- Worsening sleep despite good practices (evaluate stress, diet, environment)
- All-or-nothing thinking (remember: progress, not perfection)
- Skipping rest days (recovery is essential for results)
