# Stress & Sleep Plan (2 Weeks) - Alex's Personal Program

## Overview
This comprehensive 14-day program is designed to help you manage work-related stress and improve sleep quality through practical, science-backed micro-practices that fit seamlessly into your busy schedule.

## Daily Micro-Practices

### Week 1: Foundation Building

#### Day 1: Setting Intentions
**Morning (5 min):** 3-minute breathing exercise + intention setting
- Sit comfortably, breathe in for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 6
- Set one positive intention for the day
**Evening (10 min):** Digital sunset + gratitude practice
- Stop all screens 1 hour before bed
- Write down 3 things you're grateful for

#### Day 2: Body Awareness
**Morning (7 min):** Body scan wake-up
- Lie in bed for 5 minutes scanning from toes to head
- Notice tension without judgment
**Evening (8 min):** Progressive muscle relaxation
- Tense and release each muscle group for 10 seconds

#### Day 3: Breath as Anchor
**Morning (5 min):** Box breathing
- 4-4-4-4 pattern (inhale-hold-exhale-hold)
- Repeat 10 cycles
**Evening (10 min):** 4-7-8 breathing for sleep
- Inhale for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8
- Repeat 4 times before bed

#### Day 4: Mindful Transitions
**Morning (6 min):** Mindful coffee/tea ritual
- Focus entirely on the preparation and first few sips
**Evening (8 min):** Worry dump journaling
- Write down all worries, then close the journal

#### Day 5: Movement & Breath
**Morning (8 min):** Gentle stretching with breath awareness
- Simple neck, shoulder, and back stretches
**Evening (10 min):** Walking meditation (indoor or outdoor)
- Focus on each step and breath coordination

#### Day 6: Weekend Reset
**Morning (10 min):** Extended meditation
- Use a guided app or sit in silence
**Evening (15 min):** Self-care ritual
- Bath, reading, or hobby time

#### Day 7: Reflection & Planning
**Morning (5 min):** Week 1 reflection
- What worked? What was challenging?
**Evening (10 min):** Week 2 preparation
- Set intentions for the coming week

### Week 2: Integration & Expansion

#### Day 8: Stress Resilience
**Morning (7 min):** Resilience visualization
- Imagine handling today's challenges with calm confidence
**Evening (8 min):** Stress release body scan
- Focus on releasing tension from work stress

#### Day 9: Cognitive Reframing
**Morning (5 min):** Positive affirmations
- "I can handle whatever comes my way today"
**Evening (10 min):** Challenge unhelpful thoughts
- Identify one stressful thought and reframe it

#### Day 10: Energy Management
**Morning (6 min):** Energy assessment + intention
- Rate your energy 1-10, set realistic expectations
**Evening (8 min):** Energy restoration breathing
- Alternate nostril breathing for balance

#### Day 11: Workplace Mindfulness
**Morning (5 min):** Commute meditation
- Practice mindful driving/transit
**Evening (10 min):** Work stress decompression
- Mentally "leave work at work" ritual

#### Day 12: Social Connection
**Morning (7 min):** Loving-kindness meditation
- Send good wishes to yourself and others
**Evening (8 min):** Connect with loved ones
- Mindful conversation or text check-in

#### Day 13: Weekend Integration
**Morning (15 min):** Longer practice of your favorite technique
**Evening (20 min):** Comprehensive self-care session

#### Day 14: Future Visioning
**Morning (10 min):** Reflect on progress and plan continuation
**Evening (10 min):** Celebrate your commitment and growth

## Sleep Hygiene Checklist

### Environment Setup
- [ ] Room temperature between 65-68°F (18-20°C)
- [ ] Blackout curtains or eye mask
- [ ] White noise machine or earplugs if needed
- [ ] Comfortable mattress and pillows
- [ ] Remove electronic devices from bedroom
- [ ] Use dim, warm lighting 2 hours before bed

### Pre-Sleep Routine (60-90 minutes before bed)
- [ ] Set a consistent bedtime (± 15 minutes)
- [ ] No caffeine after 2 PM
- [ ] No large meals 3 hours before bed
- [ ] No alcohol 4 hours before bed
- [ ] Complete work tasks and planning
- [ ] Take a warm shower or bath
- [ ] Practice relaxation technique
- [ ] Read fiction or listen to calming music

### Sleep Quality Optimization
- [ ] Wake up at the same time daily (including weekends)
- [ ] Get 15-30 minutes of morning sunlight
- [ ] Limit daytime naps to 20 minutes before 3 PM
- [ ] Keep a sleep diary
- [ ] If awake for 20+ minutes, leave bed and do quiet activity

## Managing Work Stress

### Workplace Micro-Breaks (2-3 minutes each)
1. **Desk Breathing:** 10 deep breaths at your desk
2. **Shoulder Release:** Roll shoulders and stretch neck
3. **Eye Rest:** Look at distant object for 20 seconds
4. **Mindful Sip:** Take 5 mindful sips of water
5. **Positive Self-Talk:** One encouraging phrase

### CBT-Lite Reframes for Common Work Stressors

#### "I have too much to do"
**Reframe:** "I have multiple priorities. I'll focus on the most important one right now."

#### "My boss/colleague is impossible"
**Reframe:** "This person is having their own challenges. I can only control my responses."

#### "I'm behind schedule"
**Reframe:** "I'm making progress. I'll adjust my plan and communicate if needed."

#### "I made a mistake"
**Reframe:** "Mistakes are learning opportunities. I'll address this and move forward."

#### "Work is overwhelming"
**Reframe:** "I can break this down into smaller, manageable steps."

### Emergency Stress Response (1-2 minutes)
1. **STOP Technique:**
   - **S**top what you're doing
   - **T**ake a deep breath
   - **O**bserve your thoughts and feelings
   - **P**roceed with intention

2. **5-4-3-2-1 Grounding:**
   - 5 things you can see
   - 4 things you can touch
   - 3 things you can hear
   - 2 things you can smell
   - 1 thing you can taste

### Boundary Setting Strategies
- Practice saying "Let me check my calendar and get back to you"
- Use "I'm focused on X project today" to redirect requests
- Schedule "focus time" blocks in your calendar
- Take actual lunch breaks away from your desk
- Communicate your availability clearly

## Tracking & Adjustments

### Daily Check-In Questions (2 minutes)
**Morning:**
- Energy level (1-10)
- Stress level (1-10)
- Sleep quality (1-10)
- Today's intention

**Evening:**
- How did I handle stress today?
- What worked well?
- What would I adjust tomorrow?
- Gratitude moment

### Weekly Assessment (10 minutes every Sunday)

#### Sleep Metrics
- Average bedtime/wake time
- Sleep quality average
- Number of times awakened
- Morning alertness level

#### Stress Management
- Most effective technique this week
- Biggest stress trigger
- Best moment of calm/peace
- Workplace stress level (1-10)

#### Progress Indicators
- Consistency with practices (%)
- Mood improvement (1-10)
- Energy levels (1-10)
- Overall life satisfaction (1-10)

### Adjustment Guidelines

#### If Sleep Isn't Improving:
- Review bedroom environment
- Check caffeine timing
- Consider medical consultation
- Adjust bedtime routine timing

#### If Stress Remains High:
- Increase micro-break frequency
- Try different breathing techniques
- Examine workload and boundaries
- Consider professional support

#### If Consistency Is Challenging:
- Reduce practice time temporarily
- Focus on just morning OR evening routine
- Use phone reminders/apps
- Find accountability partner

### Customization Notes
- Adapt timing based on your schedule
- Substitute activities that don't resonate
- Increase difficulty gradually
- Focus on progress, not perfection

### Success Metrics After 2 Weeks
- [ ] Established consistent sleep schedule

- [ ] Reduced work stress impact on personal time
- [ ] Improved morning energy levels
- [ ] Enhanced ability to transition between work and rest
- [ ] Created sustainable daily routine
- [ ] Built awareness of stress triggers and responses

5. Maintain sleep hygiene habits permanently
6. Schedule monthly check-ins with yourself
